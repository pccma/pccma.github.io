Portland Chinese Christian & Missionary Alliance

As a multicultural church, we have services in English, Cantonese, and Mandarin. We believe in serving the local community by organizing events such as VBS and Saturday Chinese school. We believe in reviving the city by preaching the Gospel and sharing God's love.

Who We Are

ENGLISH SERVICE

English services begin every Sunday from 9:15am - 10:30am. Pastor Ken Carlson leads this.

CANTONESE SERVICE

Cantonese service begins every Sunday from 11:00am - 12:15pm. Pastor Philip Huynh leads this.

MANDARIN SERVICE

Mandarin service begins every Sunday from 9:15am - 10:30am in the Growing Hall. Led by John and Queenie Lau.

SUNDAY SCHOOL

English Sunday school begins after service at 11:00am - 12:00 pm. Chinese Sunday School begins vice versa.
